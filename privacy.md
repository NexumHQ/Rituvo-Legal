 # Privacy Policy

  **Effective Date: February 18, 2026**

  ## 1. Information We Collect

  **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and name. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information from those services.

  **User Content:** Journal entries, saved verses, prayer history, and preferences you create within the App are stored to provide the service.

  **Usage Data:** We collect basic usage information such as reading progress and streak data to power App features.

  ## 2. How We Use Your Information

  - To provide and maintain the App's features
  - To sync your data across devices
  - To manage your subscription
  - To send notifications you have opted into (reminders, streaks)

  ## 3. Data Storage

  Your data is stored securely using Supabase (cloud database) and locally on your device. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

  ## 4. Third-Party Services

  The App uses the following third-party services:

  - **Supabase** — authentication and data storage
  - **RevenueCat** — subscription management
  - **Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In** — authentication
  - **Expo** — app infrastructure

  Each service has its own privacy policy governing its use of data.

  ## 5. Data Sharing

  We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (e.g., payment processing through Apple).

  ## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

  You can delete your account at any time from within the App. When you delete your account, all your personal data and content is permanently removed from our servers.

  ## 7. Children's Privacy

  The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

  ## 8. Your Rights

  You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can do this through the App's profile settings or by contacting us.

  ## 9. Changes to This Policy

  We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

  ## 10. Contact

  For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at rituvoapp@gmail.com.
